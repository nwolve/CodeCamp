Making Fermented Bread

From Thin Air

My second sourdough loaf

But First...

You need to grow yeast.

What you'll need to grow your yeast:

  • Clear jar
  • 40g purified water
  • 40g Flour
  • rubber spatula

In a clear jar (mason jar will do, but look for one that has straight and even sides from the bottom to the top) add 40 grams flour and 40 grams H2O. Specifically H20 without any chlorine, this means no tap water.

Feed your yeast (daily)

Each day at the same time, remove enough contents from your jar that you jar weighs 40g more than what it did with nothing in it.

Then add 40g flour and 40g H2O (just like you did yesterday
